Teacher’s Message

Welcome to KMC Samulnori. Our team is a place where young performers learn more than rhythm and movement. Through every rehearsal, students practice patience, responsibility, focus, and respect for one another. Samulnori asks each performer to listen carefully, respond confidently, and understand how an individual part contributes to the strength of the whole group.

We work to preserve the energy and spirit of traditional Korean percussion while helping every member grow at a comfortable and meaningful pace. Some students arrive with musical experience, while others begin by holding an instrument for the first time. What matters most is their willingness to learn, support their teammates, and continue improving through practice.

Performances give our members an opportunity to share Korean culture with the wider community. Behind every moment on stage are many hours of repetition, teamwork, correction, laughter, and determination. I am proud of the care our students show toward the music and toward each other, and I hope every audience can feel the joy and unity they create together.
