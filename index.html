Four Instruments. One Voice.

Each instrument represents a unique element of Korean traditional music.

Janggu

The hourglass drum creating expressive melodies and rhythms.

Buk

The deep drum providing power and foundation.

Jing

A powerful gong representing the sound of wind.

Kkwaenggwari

The bright leading gong that guides the ensemble.
