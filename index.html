The Four Instruments

🥁 Janggu

The iconic hourglass drum capable of both soft and powerful rhythms.

🥁 Buk

A deep bass drum providing strength and foundation for the ensemble.

🔔 Jing

A large gong creating rich, resonant sounds representing the wind.

✨ Kkwaenggwari

A small brass gong with bright sounds that lead the group.
