Our Mission

KMC Samulnori exists to preserve and share the beauty of Korean traditional percussion while inspiring the next generation through music, culture, and performance.

Four Instruments.
One Voice.

Each instrument carries a unique sound and purpose within the Samulnori ensemble.

Janggu

The hourglass drum creating expressive melodies and rhythms.

Buk

The deep drum providing strength and foundation.

Jing

A powerful gong representing the sound of wind.

Kkwaenggwari

The bright leading gong guiding the ensemble.

From Tradition
To Tomorrow

Born from Korean cultural heritage, Samulnori represents celebration, community, and the power of rhythm. KMC continues this tradition by bringing these sounds to schools, festivals, and communities.
